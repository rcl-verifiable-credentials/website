What are Verifiable Credentials ?

  • VCs are digital files that are issued to holders to recognize their achievements
  • They are secured by asymmetric cryptographic keys to prevent fraud and tampering
  • VCs are machine-readable and can be interchanged among computer systems
  • Holders can stack VCs from different issuers into collections to demonstrate their achievements in occupational areas
  • Holders store their collections and VCs in mobile and web digital wallets
  • Holders can easily share their collections and VCs with employers and peers on the web, social media and on their mobile devices
  • VCs are part of a vibrant eco-system of issuers, holders and verifiers that all contribute to the VC's relevance and fitness for purpose
